To begin with, you have to log in via your WAX wallet and choose a nickname. Right after that, you’ll receive the welcome bonus.

1

Now, you are in the main menu. Here you can view your NFT card collection, previous and ongoing battles and visit the Arena!

2

Here’s the Arena!

There are five battle panels on the screen. Ferocious rats fight against our knights. Unfortunately, without you, evil often wins.

Do not despair: you can always add your NFT card to the knight by clicking on the knight’s '+' to increase the overall performance.

3

Also, you can pay coins to increase one of the indicators of a friendly warrior by clicking on the “Update” button.

4

Now, you can click “To battle”: The bet amount will be deducted from your account, the panel will freeze and if your knight wins after the countdown ends, you get double the amount of your bet as a reward.

5

Every card, including the NFT ones, has its own elemental class, which is responsible for the attack and damage taken.

There are five classes in total: normal, fire, water, earth, and air.
